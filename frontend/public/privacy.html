🌶️ SPYCE Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

SPYCE ("we", "our", or "us") operates the SPYCE application accessible via Pi Browser. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use SPYCE:

  • Pi Username: Provided by Pi Network when you authenticate
  • Pi User ID: A unique identifier from Pi Network
  • Videos and content you choose to upload
  • Usage data: Likes, comments, follows, and marketplace activity

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the SPYCE platform
  • To process Pi Network payments and transactions
  • To display your content to other users
  • To calculate and distribute Pi rewards
  • To improve our services

3. Pi Network Integration

SPYCE uses the Pi Network SDK for authentication and payments. We only use Pi SDK for login — no other authentication methods are used. All payment transactions are processed through Pi Network's blockchain.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Pi Network (for authentication and payments)
  • Cloudinary (for video and image storage)
  • MongoDB Atlas (for database storage)

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including JWT authentication, HTTPS encryption, and secure cloud storage to protect your data.

7. Children's Privacy
